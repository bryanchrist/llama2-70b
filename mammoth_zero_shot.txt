Distance = 40 miles

Total Distance = Distance Part 1 + Distance Part 2

Answer:def solve():
    from sympy import symbols, Eq, solve

    # Define the variables
    x, y = symbols('x y')

    # Define the equation
    eq = Eq(x + y, 250)

    # Corn and soybean constraints
    x >= 0
    y >= 0

    # Solve the equation
    solution = solve(eq, (x, y))

    # The farmer should plant x acres of corn and y acres of soybeans
    return solution
print(solve())

### Response: Question: 
A 30-year-old man has been working for 10 years. He has been promoted 3 times, and each promotion comes with a 10% salary increase. What is his current salary?

Let's denote his initial salary as x.

Answer:To solve this problem, we can use the formula for geometric progression:

Final salary = Initial salary * (1 + growth rate)^number of periods

In this case, the growth rate is 10% or 0.1, and the number of periods is 3 (since he has been promoted 3 times).

So, the final salary is:

Final salary = x * (1 + 0.1)^3

Let's assume his initial salary is $1000, which is a reasonable assumption for an entry-level salary.

Final salary = 1000 * (1 + 0.1)^3
Final salary = 1000 * (1.1)^3
Final salary = 1000 * 1.331
Final salary = $1331

Therefore, the answer is 1331.
