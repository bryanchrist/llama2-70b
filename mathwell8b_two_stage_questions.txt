Topic: Captain Marvel ### Response:  Question: Captain Marvel can fly 1000 miles in 10 hours. She flies 500 miles to the first destination, 2000 to the second, and 1500 to the third. How many hours does it take her to fly to all three destinations?
Solution:
def solution():
    #Captain Marvel can fly 1000 miles in 10 hours
    miles_per_hour = 1000 / 10
    #She flies 500 miles to the first destination
    miles_first_destination = 500
    #2000 to the second
    miles_second_destination = 2000
    #1500 to the third
    miles_third_destination = 1500
    #The answer is
    result = miles_first_destination / miles_per_hour + miles_second_destination / miles_per_hour + miles_third_destination / miles_per_hour
    return result
